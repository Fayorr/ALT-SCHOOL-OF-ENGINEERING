Ofada Rice and Sauce

Ofada Rice is a local Nigerian brown rice variety with a distinctive nutty flavor and slightly chewy texture. It's unpolished, giving it a natural brown color and higher nutritional value than regular white rice. Ofada Sauce (also called Ayamase or Designer Stew) is a spicy green pepper sauce that traditionally accompanies ofada rice. Made with green bell peppers, scotch bonnet peppers, locust beans (iru), assorted meats, and palm oil, it has a rich, smoky flavor with a characteristic greenish color. Together, ofada rice and sauce make a beloved traditional Nigerian dish that's both flavorful and culturally significant, often served at special occasions and local eateries called "buka."

Recipe Information:

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 12 minutes
  • Servings: 20 Ladle soups
  • Difficulty: Beginner

Tips to Note

  1. Bleaching Oil can be a dangerous enviorment, be careful!
